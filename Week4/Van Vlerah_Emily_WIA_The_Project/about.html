About Emily's Pizza!


We started out in Atlanta, GA in 2007 as a small pizzaria. Always making the freshes pizzas with local bought goods to support local business owners. After a few years we desides to open a second location in Athens, GA in 2009. Business was amazing between the town cites that we choose to expand to the amazing city of Savannah. We have and will continue to get out ingredience from local business owners and it will always be the freshes ingredience garenteed.



We always use a brick over to bake and cook all of our pizzas, Calzones, and Panini.
